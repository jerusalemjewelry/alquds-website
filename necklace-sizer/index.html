How to Find Your Necklace Size

Method 1: Measure an Existing Necklace
Take a necklace that hangs exactly the way you like it. Lay it straight on a flat surface and measure its length from the outer edge of the clasp to the end ring using a standard tape measure or ruler.

Method 2: Wrap a Soft Tape Measure
Wrap a flexible sewing tape measure around your neck, holding it at the desired position where you want the necklace or pendant to rest. Note the measurement in inches or centimeters.
