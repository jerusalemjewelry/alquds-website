Policies

Shipping Policy

At Alquds Jewelry, we understand the incredible importance of delivering high-value goods safely to your door. All our shipments are securely handled, tracked, and fully insured against loss or damage.

Processing Times

Order processing times depend on the item type:

  • In-Stock Items: Processed and shipped within 1-2 business days.
  • Custom Name Plates: Made-to-order and take approximately 7-14 business days before shipment.
  • Backorder Items: Specific timings will be communicated to you directly.

Delivery

All orders are shipped with an insured carrier for a flat rate of $50.00. Because of the high value of gold and diamond jewelry, we require an adult signature upon delivery to ensure it arrives safely into your hands.

Domestic shipments in the United States generally take 2-4 business days to arrive after processing.

For your security, we will only ship to the Shipping Address verified during checkout via PayPal. Orders flagged for very high risk by fraud detection may be automatically canceled and refunded.

Exchanges

We take pride in the quality of our craftsmanship. While we do not offer returns or refunds, if you are not completely satisfied with your ready-made purchase, we accept exchange requests within 7 days of delivery.

Exchange Conditions

  • The item must be completely unworn, undamaged, and exactly in the same condition as when it was shipped.
  • All original packaging, tags, certificates, and boxes must be included unblemished.

Non-Exchangeable Items

Due to the personalized or investment nature of some items, the following are Final Sale and cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Customized items (e.g., Name Plates, engraved jewelry, specific size alterations).
  • Items heavily modified or resized by someone else after purchase.
  • Coins & Bullions: Due to continuous fluctuations in the global precious metal market, all investments in Coins and Bullions are final sale.

Initiate an Exchange

If your order is eligible for an exchange, please Contact Us immediately within the 7-day window. Do not mail an item back without first receiving authorization. Strict packing instructions will be provided to ensure insurance coverage for your exchange. Please note that customers are responsible for covering all return shipping costs.
