Our Heritage

A Legacy of Craftsmanship

Welcome to Alquds Jewelry, proudly family-owned and operated for over 20 years. We specialize in bringing the finest traditions of Middle Eastern luxury directly to you. From our exquisite 21k Gold collections to our breathtaking diamond pieces, every item in our showroom is selected with passion and a deep respect for our heritage.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to preserve the authentic craftsmanship of our ancestors while offering unparalleled quality to our local community in Bridgeview, Illinois. Today, we bring that same intimate, family-first approach to our online store, serving clients across the country who appreciate true elegance.

Whether you are searching for a custom nameplate, an imported gold bangle, or investing in bullion, we treat every customer like family.
