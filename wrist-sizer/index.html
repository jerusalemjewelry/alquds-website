How to Measure Your Wrist

  1. Wrap a soft tape measure or strip of paper around your wrist bone snugly but not tight.
  2. If using paper, mark where the ends meet and measure that length with a standard ruler.
  3. Take that base measurement and add **0.5 inches (1.27 cm)** to find your standard **Comfort Fit** bracelet size.

How to Measure Your Hand for Bangles

  1. Bring your thumb and little finger together as if you were putting on a bangle.
  2. Using a soft tape measure or paper strip, wrap it around the widest part of your hand (across your knuckles and the base of your thumb).
  3. Mark where it overlaps, measure it, and compare it to the table to find the diameter size you need.
